Chill Kill

Red Velvet

Chill Kill is the third Korean studio album and fourth overall by South Korean girl group Red Velvet released by SM Entertainment on November 13, 2023.

This album and its concept focus more on their ‘Velvet’ concept, complete with intricate lore posted across their various social media accounts.
