The Night in Question: French Exit Outtakes

TV Girl

The Night in Question: French Exit Outtakes is an EP (extended play) self-released by TV Girl May 1, 2020.

It collects eight songs which were recorded during the French Exit era, but did not make the final cut for the album.

Tracklist:

  2. Valerie
  3. Lines to a Different Play
  4. It Almost Worked
  5. The Desolation Tango

  7. Your Friends Are Calling Your Name
  8. Stay Away (It's Like That)
