Heaven or Las Vegas

Cocteau Twins

Heaven or Las Vegas is the sixth studio album by the Scottish band Cocteau Twins, released on 17 September 1990 by label 4AD.

The album blends dream pop and ambient pop with elements of psychedelia, experimental, and electronic textures.

Heaven or Las Vegas has since been recognised as one of Cocteau Twins' most influential works and as a landmark in dream pop.
